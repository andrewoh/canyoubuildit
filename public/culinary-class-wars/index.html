Booking rules

How to execute from California

  1. Install CatchTable Global now and log in before the drop.
  2. Save every restaurant page you care about before trying to book.
  3. Use two devices/accounts if Stella can help, especially for Trid and Choi Dot.
  4. At 5:55 PM PDT on a known 10:00 AM KST release day, be on the restaurant page.
  5. At exactly 6:00 PM PDT, refresh and select the first acceptable table. Do not over-optimize time slots.
  6. If sold out, set cancellation alerts for June 2–4 lunch and dinner and re-check during Seoul daytime.
  7. For phone-only restaurants, use your hotel concierge and give them Korean restaurant name, date, party size, and backup times.
